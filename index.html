Who am I?

Ardent politics buff, frustrated lover of literature, tech geek, die-hard quizzer, Formula 1 fanatic, and a hard rock fan. In the immortal words of the existential philosopher, Popeye the Sailor Man --

"I yam what I yam and that's all what I yam"

Background

Work in academia - I am an Assistant Professor of English (with a stubborn resistance to disciplinary specialisation) at Royal Global University, Guwahati. I am also reading for a PhD from Gauhati University with a dissertation focused on Post-Soviet immigration fiction. The working title of my dissertation is - "Hybrid Cultures, Plural Identities: De-constructing the Hyphen in Contemporary Russian-American Literature".

Prior to this, I did my BA and MA in English from Tezpur University,Assam. My undergraduate term paper was on Gustave Flaubert's Madame Bovary and my postgraduate dissertation was on the plays of Mahesh Dattani ("Sexuality and Segregation in Select Plays of Mahesh Dattani").
