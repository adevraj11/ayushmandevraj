Welcome to my personal homepage - or as I like to call it - my digital fortress of solitude.


Who am I?

I'm an ardent politics buff, a frustrated lover of literature, tech geek, die-hard quizzer, Formula 1 fanatic, and a hard rock fan. In the immortal words of the existential philosopher, Popeye the Sailor Man --

"I yam what I yam and that's all what I yam"

Background

Work in academia - I am an Assistant Professor of English (with a stubborn resistance to disciplinary specialisation) at Royal Global University, Guwahati. I am also reading for a PhD from Gauhati University with a dissertation focused on Post-Soviet immigration fiction. The working title of my dissertation is - "Hybrid Cultures, Plural Identities: De-constructing the Hyphen in Contemporary Russian-American Literature".
